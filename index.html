Popular destinations

Burgas

Are you looking for a summer destination for 2022 already? Do you wish to visit a place, bursting at the seams with beautiful nature and a lot of cute homeless cats? Then Burgas might be just the city for you! Burgas, also known as the “The marine capital of Bulgaria”, can offer you plentiful of amazing views and even more amazing food! So, what are you waiting for? Book your ticket to Burgas now by clicking here!

Nesebar

Perhaps you are looking for something smaller and quieter? Nesebar is the right spot for you to visit! The old town of Nesebar is built on the remains of an old Thracian seaport and thus offers many historical sites to visit! But perhaps you are not in the mood to look at old buildings and such? You wish to party? Well luckily for you Sunny Beach is only a couple of bus stops away! You can book your trip to Nesebar here!

Sunny Beach

So, you want to party? You want to spend your summer trip while dancing and singing? Does Bulgaria have the place for you! With its numerous bars, clubs and hotels, Sunny Beach is the place for you! Situated only a couple of kilometers from Nesebar, it’s the perfect place to party your summer days away! You can even head to the beach if you get tired of the clubs. Book your trip to Sunny Beach here!
